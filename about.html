About SamLisa Energy

A local, customer-focused energy partner helping Victorian homes and businesses stay comfortable and efficient.

Who We Are

SamLisa Energy is an energy solutions company based in Victoria, Australia. We specialise in heating, cooling, solar, battery storage, and EV charging for both residential and commercial customers.

Our team blends practical, on-site experience with up-to-date product knowledge. We take the time to understand your property, listening carefully to your needs before recommending any system.

Our Mission

To make high-quality, efficient energy solutions accessible and understandable for everyday Australians—delivered with integrity, professionalism, and care.

Our Values

The principles that guide every project we deliver.

Honesty & Transparency

Clear explanations, realistic expectations, and upfront pricing—no confusing jargon or hidden surprises.

Quality Workmanship

Attention to detail in design, installation, and finishing so your system runs smoothly for years.

Customer Care

Respect for your time, property, and comfort—from the first conversation to post-install support.

Why Customers Choose SamLisa.

  • Tailored system design instead of one-size-fits-all packages
  • Careful installation by experienced technicians
  • Responsive support and clear communication

Service Promise
